Features of Data Tools

Welcome to Data Tools, your all-in-one solution for managing and analyzing your company's data. Below, we outline the key features and steps to get started.

How It Works

  1. Create Your Company

    As an admin, create a company profile that will serve as the hub for your data, team members, and tools.

  2. Customize Your Database

    No external files needed. Fill in sales entries on the website and get real-time analysis instantly.

  3. Manage Team Roles

    Invite teammates, assign roles like Admin, Moderator, or Viewer, and control access with precision.

  4. Analyze Your Data

    Visualize insights, track performance, and make data-driven decisions with built-in tools.

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

We implement robust RBAC to secure your data and manage your team with ease.

  • Website-Wide Roles

    • Admin: Full control across the site (app creator)
    • Subscriber: Access to blogs, news, and updates (no company creation)
    • Viewer: View public content only

  • Company-Specific Roles

    • Admin: Control your companyâ€™s data, team, and settings
    • Moderator: Manage data/team, but not settings
    • Viewer: View-only access

Getting Started

Take control of your data in just a few steps:

  1. Sign up for an account
  2. Create your company profile
  3. Add data entries directly via our interface
  4. Invite team members and assign roles
  5. Use analysis tools for real insights
